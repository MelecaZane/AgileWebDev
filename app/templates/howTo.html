Step 1: Sign Up or Sign In

Welcome to LFG! Follow the steps below to get started:

  • If you're new to LFG, Sign Up and create an account.
  • If you already have an account, Sign In to access your LFG profile.
  • This can be located on the top right of the screen

Step 2: Access Your Profile

Once you log in, you will see your username that you signed up with on the top right of the screen.

Step 3: Access Create Post

You can access creating a new post on the top left of the screen.

Step 4: Creating a Post

To create a post, you can select a platform, the game, specify the number of players needed, and provide a title for your post.

Step 5: Replying/Joining a Group

After the post is created, players can join the group. Other players can sign up to the group by clicking the blue join button.

Step 6: Enjoy the LFG Website

Enjoy using the LFG website to connect with other players and find your next gaming adventure!
